Rising Dragon Gamers is a gaming group locted in Bathurst, Nsw. The Rising Dragon Gamer group meets once a week and consists of members aged 13 and up. The games we play include Dungeons and Dragons, board games, online games, adventures and quests. We encourage everyone of any skill level to join the group. We look forward to meeting you at one of our social events!
