Who are we?

Rising Dragon Gamers are a gaming group based in Bathurst, NSW. We group of people varying of the ages of 13^ that meet on a weekly basis to play a variety of games to suit everyone. Games include board games, online games, adventures, and Quests. To find more information regarding the games that are played, check out our gallery of games or come into our store at 2 Dry Street, Bathurst NSW 2795.
